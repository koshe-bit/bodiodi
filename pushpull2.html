THE MESH IS FELT,
NOT SHOWN

The goal here is not to draw a system of nodes and lines. The goal is to create the sensation that something beneath the surface is organizing tension — gathering inward, compressing slightly, then easing back out. More fascia than diagram. More body than interface.

Cursor movement activates a hidden field in the hero. On touch devices or at rest, the field drifts gently on its own. This is a restrained first pass meant to test feeling before form.
