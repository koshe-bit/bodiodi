PRESSURE, NOT POINTER

This version removes the visible cursor entirely inside the hero and tries to let the page surface communicate the interaction. Instead of seeing a tool move around, you should feel compression, inward pull, and a slight yielding of the content under that force.

If this still reads too much like a decorative effect and not enough like contraction, the next move would be to increase band compression and reduce the radial pressure layer even further.
